Privacy Policy

BabyPulse app · last updated: 23 June 2026

This privacy policy explains what data the BabyPulse app (the “App”) processes and how we protect it. By using the App you accept the rules described below.

In short: The App stores the data you enter (e.g. feedings, sleep, your child’s name and date of birth) in your account so it is available across your devices. We do not sell data and do not use it for advertising.

1. Data controller

The data controller is the app developer. Contact for privacy matters: jaroslaw1980@gmail.com.

2. What data we process

  • Account data: e-mail address and account identifier (when signing in with e-mail or Google).
  • Child data you enter: name, date of birth, sex.
  • Activity data: feeding records (amount, time), sleep records, goals, settings.
  • Contact data (optional): when you use the contact form — name, e-mail and message content.
  • Technical data: basic device/browser information needed for the App to work and for diagnostics.

3. Purposes and legal basis

  • Providing the App’s features (saving and showing your data) — performing the service at your request.
  • Authentication and account security.
  • Handling messages sent via the contact form.
  • Sharing your child’s data with the other parent — only after voluntarily joining with an invite code.

4. Sharing with other parents

The App lets two parents keep a shared diary. The other parent gains access to the child’s data only after deliberately entering an invite code you share. Data is not shared with anyone else.

5. Service providers (processors)

We use trusted third-party services only to the extent necessary for the App to function:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) — sign-in and secure storage of your data. Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Formspree — delivering contact-form messages. Policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • AI assistant (Puter) — optional chat. Questions you type in the chat are sent to the AI service to generate a reply. Do not enter data you do not wish to share.

6. Data retention

We keep data for as long as you have an account in the App. Data is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS).

7. Your rights

  • Access and correction of your data (in the App settings).
  • Deletion of data/account — write to jaroslaw1980@gmail.com and we will delete your data.
  • Objection to or restriction of processing.

8. Children

The App is intended for parents and caregivers (adults). It is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children as users. Child data (name, date of birth) is entered by an adult caregiver.

9. Informational nature

The App is informational and supportive only. The norms, analyses and AI assistant answers do not constitute medical advice and do not replace a consultation with a pediatrician.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The current version is always available at this address, with the date of the last update.
