 ENGLISH (en-US / en-GB)
------------------------------------------------------------

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Baby feeding, sleep and growth tracker with an AI assistant.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
BabyPulse is a modern diary for parents — track your baby’s feeding,
sleep and growth in one simple place.

🍼 FEEDING
• Quickly log feeds (milk amount + time)
• Live timer since the last feed
• Forecast of the next feed based on your baby’s rhythm
• History and entry editing

💤 SLEEP
• Live sleep timer (start/stop)
• Suggested wake and nap windows matched to age
• Age-based sleep goal and history

📊 SUMMARY & ANALYSIS
• Daily goal, day/night comparison, 7-day trend
• Hourly heatmap, daily/weekly/monthly charts

🤖 AI ASSISTANT
• Analysis of rhythm, appetite and sleep
• AI chat — ask anything about your baby

👪 FOR BOTH PARENTS
• Shared diary — the other parent joins with an invite code
• Data securely in the cloud, available on every device

🌐 5 LANGUAGES: Polish, English, German, French, Italian
🎨 Light and dark theme
🔔 Reminders for the upcoming feed

⚕️ The app is informational and does not replace a pediatrician’s advice.
